More about me!

Hello! I'm Ignacio Fianza, born in 2007 and currently residing in the beautiful country of Uruguay. Ever since I can remember, I've been passionate about technology and the endless possibilities it offers. My journey into the world of computing started early, fueled by constant curiosity and a desire to create and innovate.


My English proficiency is certified at A1, and I am actively working to improve my language skills while continuing with my studies and projects.


I am currently a student at UTU (Universidad del Trabajo del Uruguay) in the Informatics program. UTU, known for its focus on technical and vocational education, has provided me with a solid foundation in computer science principles and practical skills. Through this program, I've gained a deep understanding of various aspects of computing, from software development to systems administration.


To further enhance my skills and knowledge, I am also enrolled in a Full-Stack Development course at DevSchool Academy (Argentina). This intensive program has allowed me to dive deeper into web development, mastering both front-end and back-end technologies. From creating dynamic and efficient web applications to optimizing performance and designing innovative user interactions, this course has been instrumental in shaping my abilities as a developer.


In addition to my passion for technology, I have a strong interest in motorsport and simracing. I follow various forms of racing, including Formula 1, Touring Car Racing, Turismo Carretera, the World Endurance Championship, IMSA, and Formula Drift. Simracing, in particular, allows me to experience the thrill of racing from the comfort of my home, combining my love for cars with my interest in simulation and competition.
